Brand Guidelines

Clear Space

Maintain minimum 16px clear space around logo on all sides to ensure visibility and impact.

Minimum Size

Never use logo smaller than 32px width to preserve legibility and mark details.

Color Variants

Primary teal for digital. Single color (black/white) for monochrome applications and print.

Background

Works on light and dark backgrounds. Ensure minimum 4:5:1 contrast ratio for accessibility.
